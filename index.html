My latest work

Then & Again

I myself am a big fan of second hand vintage clothing shops. Today, fast fashion has invaded the fashion industry and selling and buying clothes from others is a new great game. Finding unique and old vintage items that probably only a few still have is something that some people look for when buying on apps like Then & Again.

My Drawer

My drawer facilitates the shopping experience for users who prefer to buy from home or anywhere at any time while they save many trips to different stores looking for what they need. The drawer has it, open an account, search for what you are looking for (save it for any time you want to buy it ) buy within a click and you will receive within 5 days.
