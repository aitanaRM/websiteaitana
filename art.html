ARTE á Paris

As a UI student at Career Foundry, I was assigned to create a native mobile app for Android and iOS. My goals was to develop an intuitive, user-friendly art app that would help locals and tourist in Paris to be informed about all the art expositions in the city.

Process

Research

Arte á Paris has all the city’s most important and coolest art exhibitions, you won’t want to miss any. Arte á Paris is for everyone who is visiting or living in the city and wants to explore the different art expositions in Paris. The app has all the most important and coolest art exhibitions in the city, you won’t miss any. Unlike other apps,Arte á Paris offers all the events in one app to simplify and organize the users’ agenda.

Ideation

Other Apps

  • Most of them only offer expositions of their museums, not others.
  • Do not show the proper address or location of their expositions.
  • Are not up to date

ARTE á Paris

  • Arte á Paris has all the most important and coolest art expositions going on in the city, you won’t miss any.
  • Clear map of the location, time, and price of the expositions.
  • Arte á Paris shows a daily, weekly, and monthly calendar with all the expositions in Paris.
  • Like and share your experience and let other know where the best expositions are.
